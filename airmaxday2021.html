PROJECT
NIKE AIR MAX DAY WORLDWIDE 2021

ROLE
Production Designer



INFO
To help launch Nike's 2021 Air Max Day on @nikesportswear, I produced and designed the batch of social and paid-media assets.

It was a virtual experience that millions across the world would attend as artists and influencers in featured countries create a dialogue about their passions and inspirations. This was an undertaking that required real-time editing and curation of numerous UGC (user-generated content) throughout the night and day.
