COLORING + DIGITAL RESTORATION
Below are personal restorations of iconic music videos with proper attention to detail compared to the official HD versions on Youtube.

My desire to work in coloring goes back to 2014, when Buffy The Vampire Slayer was remastered in high-definition.
The series was tragically mishandled by Fox in attempt to cut costs when the team in charge failed to apply the necessary color grading that defined numerous scenes.
At the same time, Fox's The X-Files was given the proper remastering treatment that revealed gorgeous detail and color that wasn't available in its original mastering format due to technological limitations.


MADONNA - MATERIAL GIRL (1985)
DIRECTED BY MARY LAMBERT

Issues addressed: color and detail
This remaster restores the original colors of the classic red set and pink dress made iconic by Marilyn Monroe, cross-referenced using set photos and screen captures from Gentlemen Prefer Blondes.
The official Youtube video inadvertedly shifts the colors towards an orange tint in attempt to achieve a warm skin tone.

Notable achievement
My initial "Like A Prayer" restoration caught the attention and approval of Mary Lambert in early 2022, which led me to re-doing "Material Girl" with the master file that her team sent over.
GWEN STEFANI - COOL (2005)
DIRECTED BY SOPHIE MULLER

Issues addressed: aspect ratio, color, and detail
This remaster provides way more detail and a vertically expanded image while keeping proportions accurate to the source material.
The official Youtube video has been stretched making their faces appear wider than normal with little to no detail.
KYLIE MINOGUE - COME INTO MY WORLD (2002)
DIRECTED BY MICHEL GONDRY

Issues addressed: color and detail
This remaster retains more detail and keeps the image's integrity intact.
The official Youtube video has oversharpened edges, destroying detail and adding haloing and anti-aliasing artifacts.
