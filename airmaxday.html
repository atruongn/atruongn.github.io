PROJECT
Air Max Day Worldwide 2021
TYPE
Social media launch
INFO
I produced a series of social and paid-media assets to help launch Nike's 2021 Air Max Day on @nikesportswear. It was a singular live event that millions across the world would tune into to watch artists and influencers in featured countries as they start a dialogue about their passions and inspirations. This was an undertaking that required real-time editing and curation of UGC (user-generated content) throughout the entire day.
